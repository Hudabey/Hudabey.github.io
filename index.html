Research

AR Video Generation Inference Profiling

● active

End-to-end latency and memory profiling of MAGI-1 and SkyReels-V2 across 2s–30s video lengths. Decomposing time into text encoding, autoregressive chunk denoising, attention/feedforward/normalization, KV cache ops, and VAE decoding. Looking for non-linear scaling and bottlenecks that current work misses.

Quantization Robustness in Distilled Diffusion Models

● active

Why do different distillation methods respond so differently to INT8 quantization? Running controlled ablations with SageAttention across rCM, LCM, and Turbo.

→ rCM maintains quality under INT8 while LCM and Turbo degrade significantly — distillation method choice has direct deployment implications

TurboDiffusion Ablation Study

done

Systematic decomposition of TurboDiffusion's optimization stack. Isolated each component's contribution to end-to-end speedup.

→ SLA alone = 2.80× DiT speedup (92% of total) — other components marginal. Contradicts original paper's additive framing.
